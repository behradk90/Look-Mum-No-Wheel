MARIASOLE GIGLIO

Mariasole creates hand-built ceramics from slab and coil, without the use of a lathe. She works with various clay, from stoneware to earthenware, coloured with engobe and then hand-decorated using techniques such as debossing, engraving and sgraffito.

Her work includes functional homewares, from pots and candle holders to plates, cups and tea-pots. Although her main creative focus is on vases, as they recalls a tradition of beauty tied with ancient roots and they preposes a challenge to aesthetics in the world of contemporany ceramics.
